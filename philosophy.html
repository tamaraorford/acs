Philosophy

Begining The Journey

I think an old dog can always learn a new trick! As a former technophobe taking a giant leap into the IT space I am now anecdotal proof of this. Learning can never be a waste of time and there is beauty in every intellectual pursuit if you look hard enough. While I am not sure what particular aspects of development I will be drawn to most I am sure my journey, now started will never stop.

Embracing failure

Learning to embrace failure and allowing for organic growth instead of rigid planning has been a paradigm shift for me in my personal approach to work projects and has encouraged me to have a much more ‘hands on’ approach to other learning discourses. After only a term of immersion in Ruby and html/css I am aware of changes in my thought processes and as I look forward to the event horizon I am excited to see what the future brings!
